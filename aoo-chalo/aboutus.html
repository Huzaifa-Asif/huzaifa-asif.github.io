Why Travel with us

We want to hear from you!

WHY TRAVEL WITH US

All you have to do is make a wish and leave the rest to us, for we help convert dreams into reality. Need to go to a quiet place away from the humdrum of city life but have a limited number of days? Travelling with kids and need to stay in child-friendly natural surroundings? Want to camp by the lake on a romantic honey moon getaway? Dream of taking your parents to the North? We got you!! Choose your destination(s), mode of transport, standard of accommodation and leave the rest to us. Let us know your preferences and we will help you curate the perfect itinerary that suits all your needs. From trekking trips to road travels one can either choose from our fixed itineraries, or select plans and destinations according to preference and let us customize them

Anyone and everyone is welcome aboard. No family is too big or small for us. We believe that families who travel together, develop stronger bonds. Therefore, we aim to create happy and long-lasting memories for everyone especially for Pakistani families living abroad who wish to take their children to Northern areas of Pakistan during vacations. They always love it especially when they travel carefree with a reliable source like Aao Chalo! Couples, whether newly married looking for a perfect romantic getaway, or old ones dreaming of a post-retirement trip, are pampered here. Special attention is given to the needs and preferences of the couples, ensuring that the time spent is fruitful and full of tender memories. A variety of plans and itineraries are also available for groups of like-minded individuals. It is often hard to find travel mates that share the same energy. You can bring your friends and colleagues, or let us know your personality type so that we group you with people with similar vibes. Additionally, as hosts from the land of hospitality, we really love to show expats and foreigners the hidden gems, and rich culture and heritage of Pakistan while ensuring their safety and comfort. So anyone can go anywhere they want to at Aao Chalo!

We prefer taking families with kids of all ages. Many people are reluctant in taking their children on long journeys and worry about their ‘boredom’. The reality is that technological advancements and monotonous routines of city life are driving children away from the outside world especially natural surroundings. Hence, they are deprived of some basic skills including the ability to accommodate, take risks and connect with mother nature. At Aao Chalo we strongly believe that Nature is the best teacher, and travelling, the best learning experience. There is a need for them to see the world without gadgets and screens and to be able to absorb nature through their five senses. So no need to worry! We got them! Our destination plans include spaces where they can be themselves and can shed their inhibitions while trying out different skills and activities designed especially for them such as climbing trees, zip-lining, rock climbing, fishing, camping etc. We keep them so engaged that after one memorable trip with us, they will beg you to go again!

Do you wish to experience the warm hospitality of Pakistani people that they are famous for? Want to stay in cozy stone walled abodes of shepherds? Or nomad tents? Ever imagine resting your head against embroidered pillows, lying over colorful kilims and listening to legends about the valley and its people? If you have fantasized about it, we will help you live it! Not only that, you can also interact with local families and engage with them by assisting them in their day to day activities such as rearing sheep, fruit picking, weaving, cooking etc. For this one-of-a-kind rich and culturally rooted experience of living and interacting with locals for one day or more, we help plan your stay with families and hosts of your preference.

We make the dining experience unforgettable! In our opinion, an entire day of travelling and sightseeing is not fully complete till it is complimented with the right kind of cuisine in the right kind of ambiance. With us you get the chance to savor original recipes not only from roadside cafes and stalls, but the opportunity to dine with locals and eat straight from the cozy comfort of their homes---the kind of food that is bound to make you forget those lavish replicated dishes served at high end hotels. From vegetarians to meat lovers, there is always something for everyone’s taste buds. Yak meat, trout, goat cheese---indulge in pure, organic food made from organic produce. Imagine waking up to a surprise platter of yellow plums or red cherries, or a basket of freshly baked wheat bread and homemade butter. Or imagine sipping on local tea or soup while watching the sunset. We promise a culinary experience so good, you are most likely to remember places because of the food you ate! That’s what we are talking about!!

You look up a destination and the most common searches about tourist hotspots show up. You tick them off as you explore the area and once you are finished, you feel content to check off the place from your bucket list. But the question is, are those the only spots that ‘make up’ a place or is there more? The answer lies in the latter question. For us, there is more to a place than just a popular lake or fort or beach. Therefore, we take our clients to roads less travelled: lesser known villages, pathways and vantage points. This adds to the entire flavor and experience. Even if you have visited a place before, we assure that you look at it with a different and changed perspective.

While we ensure that everyone gets to experience the various bounties of nature, we make double sure that the experience is internalized as well. We offer yoga retreats, meditative walks and ample space in the form of ‘me’ time to individuals so that they can develop a meaningful connection with their natural surroundings. Our plans include experiences that awaken the senses and help individuals take long and deep journeys within themselves. Traveling with Aao Chalo is the best way to heal and discover the meaning of life.

We help people do the things they want to but have always been afraid or unable to. ‘I am not fit to do this trek’, ‘How can I travel with a three-year-old?’, ‘This is a man-thing!’, ‘I cannot step out of my comfort zone’. These thoughts, fears and inhibitions are all embraced at AaoChalo. We look beyond age and gender, and make it a priority to motivate people to give their best and step out of their comfort zones to realize what their fears had been holding them back from.

One of our missions is to support and facilitate local communities, and assist in their development by empowering them. For this we hire guides and porters from the chosen destination(s). Not only does this affect the overall travelling experience of the tourist but it also aids in the growth of individuals and consequently, communities at large. The capital generated helps sustain them, making them self-reliant and progressive in the long run. We also provide access to original, traditional, handcrafted souvenirs and artefacts made by the local people at their homes, so that they get direct benefit from the purchases.
